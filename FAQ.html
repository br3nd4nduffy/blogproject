Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tarot?

Tarot is a system of divination rooted in cartomancy that uses a deck consisting of 22 Major Arcana and 56 Minor Arcana (Wands, Swords, Pentacles, and Cups.) Tarot traces its origins back to the 18th century.

Can Tarot tell the future?

That depends! While no one's future can be predicted, many people use Tarot as a means of guidance and reflection about major themes in their lives when they are looking to make a decision or consider the path ahead. Many people believe that it is a powerful tool in manifesting the energies and circumstances they'd like to see appear in their lives.

Why use Tarot for journaling?

Because Tarot is based on archetypes, and its illustrations are often which with colors and symbolism, it's a great tool for evoking strong feelings and reflecting on why they came up and what they may represent in our lives.
